ABOUT IIC

Innovation & Incubation Cell (IIC) is a student-driven community focused on creativity, innovation, and execution.
We build events, ideas, and experiences that help students explore skills beyond academics.

Vision

To create a campus culture where students innovate, collaborate, and turn ideas into real-world experiences.

Mission

  • Encourage creativity & innovation
  • Organize impactful events
  • Build strong student teams
  • Provide hands-on experience
  • Promote leadership & collaboration

Perks of Joining IIC

🎪 Work on real college events
💼 Build strong portfolio
🤝 Networking opportunities
👑 Team leadership roles
🏆 Certificates & recognition
🤹 Learn multiple skills
